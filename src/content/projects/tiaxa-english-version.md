  ## Tiaxa: Water Data Management (English Version)


  Project Description

  Motion graphics developed for Tiaxa, focusing on their "Water Data Management"
  solution. The video illustrates how the integration of IoT technology, data
  analytics, and mobile applications optimizes water resource management in
  remote mining operations. It highlights key benefits such as reduced response
  times and the elimination of human error in data collection.
